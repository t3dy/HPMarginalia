# The Relationship between Text and Woodcuts in the Hypnerotomachia Poliphili

**Roswitha Stewering** | Word & Image 14:1-2 1998

## Summary

Stewering provides a systematic analysis of how the 172 woodcut illustrations in the HP relate to the surrounding text, identifying several distinct modes of text-image interaction ranging from direct illustration to symbolic commentary. The article demonstrates that the woodcuts are not merely decorative but function as an integral interpretive layer that guides the reader's understanding of Colonna's prose. This study is foundational for understanding the HP as a multimedia artifact rather than a text that happens to be illustrated.
