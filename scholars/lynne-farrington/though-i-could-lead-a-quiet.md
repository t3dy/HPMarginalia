# Though I Could Lead a Quiet Life: The Hypnerotomachia Poliphili in English Translation

**Lynne Farrington** | Word & Image 31:2 2015

## Summary

Farrington examines the history of the HP's English translations, from the anonymous 1592 partial translation through modern attempts, analyzing how each translator navigated the extreme difficulties of Colonna's macaronic prose. The article reveals what was gained and lost in each translation, showing how translation choices shaped English-language understanding of the HP. Farrington argues that the HP's resistance to translation is itself a significant literary quality, reflecting the text's deliberate linguistic experimentalism.
