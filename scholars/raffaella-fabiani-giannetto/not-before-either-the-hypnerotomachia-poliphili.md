# Not Before Either: The Hypnerotomachia Poliphili and the Villa d'Este at Tivoli

**Raffaella Fabiani Giannetto** | Word & Image 31:2 2015

## Summary

Fabiani Giannetto examines the relationship between the HP and the famous Villa d'Este gardens at Tivoli, arguing that the garden's designer Pirro Ligorio drew on specific HP passages for the iconographic program and spatial organization of the villa's grounds. The article provides detailed comparisons between textual descriptions in the HP and physical features of the Villa d'Este. This study demonstrates the HP's concrete impact on one of the most celebrated Renaissance gardens still in existence.
