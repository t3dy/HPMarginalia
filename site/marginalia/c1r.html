Hand Primary: Pope Alexander VII (Fabio Chigi)
ery full day of visits, an encounter with one monument flows immediately into that of another.32 Having discussed Chigi’s interest in the symbolic power of images in the HP, we now proceed to his discussion of individual visual arguments in detail. The interpretation of a symbolic image first called for a decompiling of the visual assemblage into its component parts, and then the description of its composite meaning. The HP itself models this process of interpretation for the reader. ...

A digital humanities project documenting the readership and marginalia of the 1499 Aldine Hypnerotomachia Poliphili, based on James Russell's PhD thesis (Durham, 2014).
