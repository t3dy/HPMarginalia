Scholarly Significance

Page 39 shows dense but largely illegible annotations. The bottom margin's 'martem' (Mars) and 'aries' (ram) suggest an astrological reading layer alongside the architectural one. Mars and Aries are connected in traditional astrology, and both have alchemical significance (Mars = iron, Aries = calcination). If the same reader who annotated the planetary palace (p.88) also annotated this page, it would demonstrate a systematic astrological reading program across the text.
