“Timore di Polifilo”
ve experience.34 As Chigi’s argument discusses the features of the HP rhetorical persuasion, it is not unsurprising that he would note instances of pathos in the HP’s argument. This being said, the only emotion which Chigi marks is fear, with the words timore and spaventato.
