that Benedetto made use of up-to-date editions,27 and printing dates indicate that Giovio wrote these marginalia in the final two years of his life. For instance, a characteristic variant reading shows that Giovio used the 1539 Froben of Basel edition of Pliny’s Naturalis historia, which provides a terminus post quem.28 For botany, he quotes the 1542 Basel edition of LeoNaturalis historiaart Fuchs’ De historia stirpium. About This Project

A digital humanities project documenting the readership and marginalia of the 1499 Aldine Hypnerotomachia Poliphili, based on James Russell's PhD thesis (Durham, 2014).
