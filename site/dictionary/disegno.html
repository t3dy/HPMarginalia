Disegno Draft

Aesthetic Concepts
The concept of design as both drawing and intellectual conception, central to the HP's integration of text and image.
Disegno in Renaissance usage encompasses both the physical act of drawing and the intellectual faculty of design or conception. The HP's 172 woodcuts and their precise relationship to the text demonstrate disegno in action: the images are not merely illustrations but integral components of the work's meaning. The HP's influence on subsequent design culture — from garden design to emblem books to architectural treatises — stems from its demonstration that disegno operates across media.
