Book History & Bibliography
The front side of a leaf, conventionally the right-hand page when the book lies open.
Abbreviated 'r' in signature references (e.g., a1r). In the HP's original layout, most woodcuts appear on the recto. The recto is the side that would have been printed first in the press.
