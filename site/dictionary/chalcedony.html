Chalcedony Draft

Material Culture
A translucent microcrystalline quartz named among the precious stones in the HP's descriptions of luxury surfaces.
The HP catalogs precious and semi-precious stones with lapidary precision: chalcedony, jasper, agate, sardonyx, and others. These material descriptions serve both aesthetic and symbolic functions. Each stone carries traditional associations (chalcedony with eloquence and courage, jasper with healing) that add layers of meaning to the architectural surfaces Poliphilo describes. The HP's material vocabulary reflects the Renaissance fascination with natural history and the moral significance of stones.
