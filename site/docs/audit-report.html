What Still Needs Human Review

Critical

1. BL C.60.o.12 photo-to-folio mapping: Verify that sequential photo numbers

correspond to folio numbers. The BL copy is the 1545 edition; layout may differ

from the 1499 signature map by a few leaves.

2. Article summaries: All 34 summaries were generated by Claude and have not

been checked for factual accuracy, misattribution, or hallucination.

3. Hand attributions: Derived from reading Russell's prose in conversation.

The signature-to-hand mapping rules are approximate.

Important

4. Scholar metadata: Birth/death years, nationalities, and institutional

affiliations should be cross-referenced against VIAF/WorldCat.

5. Dictionary definitions: Especially the alchemical and architectural terms

should be reviewed by a domain specialist.

6. Mislabeled files: Jarzombek (De pictura, not HP) and Canone/Spruit

(Poncet on Botticelli, not emblematics) were identified by LLM and

should be confirmed.

Nice to Have

7. Timeline event dates: Some date ranges are approximate.

8. Topic cluster assignments: Some works could belong to multiple clusters.

The document_topics junction table supports this but most entries

have only one topic assigned.

About This Project

A digital humanities project documenting the readership and marginalia of the 1499 Aldine Hypnerotomachia Poliphili, based on James Russell's PhD thesis (Durham, 2014).
