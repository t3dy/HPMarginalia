Dream Narratives and Initiation Processes

University of Athens (PhD Thesis) (2016) [thesis] Dream & Religion

This brief text examines the intersection of dream narrative conventions and initiatory structures in Renaissance literature, providing context for understanding the HP's fusion of dream-vision and mystery-religion frameworks. The study situates the HP within a tradition of transformative dream narratives that goes back to classical antiquity. Though brief, it offers a useful theoretical framework for reading the HP's progressive revelation of sacred and erotic knowledge.
