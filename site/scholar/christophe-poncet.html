La scelta di Lorenzo: La Primavera di Botticelli tra poesia e filosofia

Bruniana & Campanelliana, Supplementi XXXIV (2012) [book] Text & Image

NOTE: This file is mislabeled in the corpus — the filename references Canone/Spruit and emblematics, but it actually contains Poncet's bilingual (French/Italian) study of Botticelli's Primavera. The book proposes a new reading of the painting through the iconographic tradition of the Marseille tarot's Lover card and the Choice of Hercules motif as depicted by Felice Feliciano in 1463. Poncet argues that the key to interpreting the Primavera lies in recognizing the role of the observer as a participant in the scene. This text does not engage with the HP directly.
